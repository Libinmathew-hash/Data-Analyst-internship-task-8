INSIGHTS FROM THE SALES DASHBOARD

1. The West region had the highest overall sales, while the South region consistently recorded the lowest sales.
2. Sales peaked in December, showing a strong holiday season impact, while February was the weakest month.
3. The Technology category contributed the largest share of total revenue and profit compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.
4. Profit margins were highest in Technology, while Furniture showed lower profitability despite high sales volume.
